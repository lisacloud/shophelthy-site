About Helthy

Using science and clinical research, to help you grocery shop smarter.

At Helthy, we believe nutritional labels shouldn't be a barrier to living a smarter and healthier life with diabetes. We've made it our mission to bring you the relevant, healthier facts you need to know.

How Helthy works Meet the team

Our mission

We turn dense nutritional labels into the facts that actually matter for living well with diabetes.

🔬

How we get it done

By analyzing the FDA's nutritional requirements and going through grocery retailers' food labels, we bring you the important and relevant information you need to know.

💡

Bonus points

It's also our mission to bring you insightful and useful food tips and tricks that help you avoid nutritional pitfalls.

🛒

Shop with confidence

Relevant, healthier facts — right where you shop — so a smarter, healthier life with diabetes is always within reach.
