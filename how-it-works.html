How Helthy works

Healthier choices, right where you already shop.

Helthy adds simple, at-a-glance guidance to your online grocery store — so smarter choices take zero extra effort.

✓ Helthy has been successfully added to your browser!

Three simple steps

From install to healthier shopping in under a minute.

1

Visit the online grocery shop

Head to your favorite grocery retailer's website and start shopping like you normally would. Helthy works across the major stores:

HEB Walmart Safeway Instacart Whole Foods Market Kroger
2

Sign in with the email you registered on the Helthy website

Use the same email you signed up with so Helthy can personalize your guidance.

💡 Don't know which email to use? Check your inbox and look for Helthy's email.
3

Enjoy online grocery shopping

Look for the Helthy icons to guide you toward foods that are healthier — as you browse and add to cart.
